About Me

Hello. My name is Wojtek Swiderski and I am studying Software Engineering at the University of Waterloo.

I love creating previously non-existent things with code. The areas that I find most intriguing are machine learning, computer vision and the architecture of large software systems. I have experience in application and server backend development using a variety of languages, including C++, Java, Python and Javascript.

I am currently looking for a co-op placement for the Winter term (January-April 2016). Feel free to email me if you have any questions about my resume or projects. When I'm not programming, you can find me riding the local mountain bike trails or skiing down some big hills.
