About Me

My name is Wojtek Swiderski. I am currently a Software Engineering student at the University of Waterloo. I am passionate about making great products, meeting exceptional people, and expanding my skill set. My dream is to start a Software Company that will change the way people use computers, and the way that they will live in the future.

I have an advanced knowledge of Java, C/C++, and LabView. In addition, my experience in Python and Javascript is constantly growing. Using LabView and Python, I designed robotic systems which featured technologies such as visual processing, PID control and state machines. I have a foundation in Javascript web frameworks including NodeJS (Express), BackboneJS, proficiency in Bootstrap, and experience with MongoDB and MySQL.

I am an astonishingly quick learner and pay very close attention to detail. Through my involvement in the United Nations York Catholic Assembly, I am able to efficiently lead a group towards a particular goal. I see myself as a natural leader, and take on the leadership role at any opportunity.
