About Me

I am currently a Software Engineering student at the University of Waterloo. I am passionate about making great products, meeting exceptional people, and expanding my skill set. I am a quick learner and pay very close attention to detail. Most of all, I love programming and overcoming challenging obstacles by finding new solutions to existing problems. My goal for the future is to work on innovative technologies that will move the field of software forward.

I am proficient at Java, C/C++, Python, Android Development and front-end web development in HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript. In addition, I have a working knowledge of implementing teleoperated and autonomous systems for robots using National Instruments LabView and Arduino. Also, I am comfortable with server-side web development in a UNIX environment using Apache, NodeJS, MySQL and MongoDB. Finally, I have experience with Git, Terminal and SSH.

I am most interested in cloud computing and hope to work in this field. After experimenting with different areas of computer science, I have found a passion for networks and servers. I am a strong supporter of the Unix environment and would love to work on any project that requires backend development, such as data warehousing, Android and iOS apps, and web applications.

Finally, I have strong skills in organization, leadership and project management as shown in my experience as an organizer of a Model United Nations event and as lead programmer of an FRC Robotics team. I love collaborating and spending time with other passionate people. I am an avid mountain biker and skier. I live by the saying, "Live life in moderation, including moderation."
